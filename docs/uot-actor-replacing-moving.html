UoT Actor replacing & moving


What you will need:
Utility of Time (You can download all softwares here)
OoT Debug rom (Search for it on youtube, google or where you want. The emulator Project 64 1.7 is probably the best to use with the debug rom)

The best thing to start with if you're interested in Zelda 64 modding is probably this. It's easy to do and works good. 
So the first thing you want to do when you have downloaded and unzipped UoT is creating a folder named "oot" or what ever you want, we'll come to this later. Open up Utility of Time and click on preferences > setup, here you click the "Zelda ROM to save to) to choose wich rom you want to save the modifications to. Choose your debug rom. Now click "Extract ROM". A new window will appear. Choose your debug rom and click "Ok". Now a new window will appear, choose the folder wich you made on your desktop in the beggining. A final window will popup. You don't have to do anything with it, just wait till it's done. Now you have extracted all files in to the folder! Click "Open" and navigate to the folder wich you extracted all the files to. Down in the right corner above "Open" it is written "Actors (*.zobj" click that and choose "Levels (*.zmap)". Now some other stuff will show up. Navigate to the file "spot_00_room_0.zmap" (wich is Hyrule Field) and click "open". Now choose the same, but the ".zscene" file (spot00_scene.zscene) and click open. A window will popup, just click "Ok". Now UoT should generate the map and you should be able to move around Hyrule Field! Use WASD to move and the mouse to look around. On someplaces in the map you will see small cubes with different colored sides. These are the actors we want to replace and move. Choose one of the actors that you want to replace, but be sure to not pick any that is needed (Link, cutscenes, exits etc.). I suggest you pick a "greenery" actor (To see the name of your selected actor, click "show tabs" and "actor options"). Now open the folder where UoT is, click "ext" and open the "actor_db.txt" file. Here is the Number, Variable and Models numbers for almost all actors. In this tutorial we are just going to pick a "Stalfos" for example. When you have opened the file in notepad you will see that there is a lot of numbers. Don't panic! That is just the numbers you'll need to replace actor. The "stalfos" numbers are on the top of the document, so it's really easy to find. Copy the first number and paste it over the "Number" of your actor in UoT. Copy the second number and paste it over the "Model (groups)" in your actor. Copy the third number and paste it over the "Variable" in your actor (hopefully that makes sense. If it did not, try do gain some help from the picture below). 

Now click apply. The actors name should now be stalfos, and if you save it now, you would meet a stalfos where your actor is! To move it, just drag it around with your mouse. Now click "Save" and "inject to rom". Now open your rom and choose "spot0". Go where you placed you actor and the stalfos should be there, congratulations you just did your first actor replacement!

If this tutorial did not make any sense at all there should be some videos on Youtube on how to do it.
